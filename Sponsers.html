Our Sponsors

RedWing Robotics proudly partners with organizations that share our vision for innovation, engineering excellence, and educational growth. Each sponsor contributes vital resources that empower students to bring advanced robotics to life.

Major Sponsors

Melissa ISD

Provides funding, workspace, and educational resources, allowing students to pursue high-level robotics and engineering programs within the district.

VEX Robotics

Supplies competition-grade robotics components, field equipment, and technical guidance that form the foundation of our robot’s engineering design.

Texas Instruments

Supports our team with precision electronics and mentorship, helping members strengthen programming, hardware, and analytical problem-solving skills.

Community Partners

Local businesses, families, and organizations contribute through donations, materials, and logistical support, ensuring that RedWing Robotics continues to innovate and represent Melissa at regional and national competitions.

Our Promise

Every contribution receives detailed visibility through our engineering documentation, social media recognition, and event materials. We value long-term partnerships that help students evolve into tomorrow’s engineers and leaders.
