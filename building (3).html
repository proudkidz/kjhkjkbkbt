Building Department

The Building Department is responsible for transforming CAD models into physical robots that meet competitive standards. Builders ensure every component aligns precisely with design specifications for reliability and performance under competition pressure.

Assembly and Quality

Every piece is measured and installed to exact tolerances. The team double-checks structural stability, mechanical motion, and safety before any testing begins, ensuring consistent quality in all builds.

Collaboration

The Build team works closely with CAD and Programming to ensure designs translate correctly into the final robot. By collaborating early, the team prevents fit issues and integrates key electrical and pneumatic systems seamlessly.

Testing and Iteration

After assembly, builders lead the testing phase, logging performance data, identifying faults, and refining designs. This hands-on validation process ensures the robot operates with accuracy and durability.

Safety and Training

Safety is integral to every stage of building. Students are trained in tool operation, equipment maintenance, and safe workshop conduct, creating a disciplined and secure workspace.
