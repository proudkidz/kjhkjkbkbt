CAD Department

The CAD team transforms creative concepts into precise 3D models. Using industry-grade software, they ensure accuracy and manufacturability for every component.

Design Process

Each robot begins as a digital prototype. The CAD department refines geometry, tests tolerances, and simulates motion to verify feasibility before manufacturing.

Collaboration

CAD collaborates with Building and Programming to guarantee smooth assembly and sensor placement. This ensures performance aligns with mechanical design intent.

Documentation

Detailed drawings, BOMs, and revision histories maintain traceability and allow sponsors and judges to understand RedWing’s professional design workflow.
