Marketing Department

The Marketing team manages RedWing’s brand identity through storytelling, community engagement, and sponsor relations. They ensure our engineering achievements are communicated clearly and professionally.

They lead outreach events, public demonstrations, and school showcases to promote STEM awareness and build community involvement in robotics education.

From digital graphics to video production, the marketing team maintains consistency across all materials, presenting RedWing Robotics as a forward-thinking and unified organization.

The Booth team designs immersive displays that visually represent the team’s engineering story. Interactive elements and sleek visuals make each competition experience memorable.

Collaboration with Sponsors

Marketing works directly with sponsors to showcase logos, provide media coverage, and ensure every contribution receives recognition throughout the season.
